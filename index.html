about

Hi! I'm an interaction designer working at Bank of America on the Erica/AI Team. I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2020 pursuing a B.S in Information Systems and minors in both Human Computer Interactions and Global Systems and Management.

In my few months at Bank of America, I've worked on visual ideation, animations, prototyping, conversational analysis, design systems, and defining northstar visions. I have experience as a project lead, where my goal was not just to complete the feature but also analyze my journey to present to leadership.

When I'm not wrapped around my laptop, I'm either dancing or exploring my city with my camera and friends. I have a taste for the creative, and my passions make me the person I am now. Please explore.
