I'm a senior at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a B.S in Information Systems and minors in both Human Computer Interactions and Global Systems and Management.

My experiences lie in rapid prototyping of applications and programs, aiming to not just answer a need but to help define them for the client. In short, I want to find the root causes of problems before they occur. I have numerous projects in the field of design, ranging from process design to UX/UI.

When I'm not wrapped around my laptop, I'm either dancing or exploring Pittsburgh with my camera. I have a taste for the creative, and my passions make me the person I am now. Please explore, and feel free take a detour to my flickr.
